# MedReg Product Roadmap: Empowering Ghana's Digital Health Future

This roadmap outlines the strategic evolution of MedReg, demonstrating our commitment to delivering a comprehensive, sustainable, and Ghana-owned Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system. Our phased approach ensures rapid delivery of critical functionality, continuous improvement, and alignment with the National Health Information Exchange (NHIE) mandate.

## Phase 1: Pilot & Core OPD (Months 1-6) - *Focus: Credibility & Foundation*

**Objective:** Successfully deploy a fully NHIE-compliant MVP for Outpatient Department (OPD) workflows in pilot facilities, proving MedReg's technical viability and strategic value.

### Key Deliverables:

*   **Patient Registration:**
    *   Ghana Card validation (format & Luhn checksum)
    *   NHIS number capture & validation
    *   Automated folder number generation
    *   Demographic data capture (name, DOB, gender, address, contact)
    *   NHIE Patient ID (MPI) integration & synchronization
*   **NHIS Eligibility Check:**
    *   Real-time NHIS eligibility verification via NHIE
    *   Display of active/expired status
*   **OPD Workflow:**
    *   Triage (vitals, chief complaint)
    *   Consultation (diagnosis - ICD-10, treatment plan, prescriptions)
    *   Pharmacy (dispensing, stock management integration)
    *   Billing (cash & NHIS patient handling)
*   **NHIE Integration (Core):**
    *   Patient registration submission to NHIE
    *   Encounter submission to NHIE (diagnosis, procedures)
    *   Offline queuing & synchronization mechanism
*   **Reporting:**
    *   Basic OPD register
    *   NHIS vs. Cash patient reports
    *   Top diagnoses reports
*   **User Management:**
    *   Role-based access control (Admin, Doctor, Nurse, Pharmacist, Records, Cashier)

## Phase 2: Expansion & Enhanced Workflows (Months 7-18) - *Focus: Comprehensive Care & Efficiency*

**Objective:** Expand MedReg's clinical scope to cover key inpatient and specialized services, further enhancing operational efficiency and data utility across more facilities.

### Key Deliverables:

*   **Inpatient Department (IPD) Management:**
    *   Admission & Discharge workflows
    *   Ward management & bed allocation
    *   Daily progress notes & treatment sheets
*   **Laboratory & Imaging Integration:**
    *   Order entry for lab tests & imaging
    *   Results viewing & management
    *   Basic lab instrument interfacing
*   **Antenatal Care (ANC) Module:**
    *   Ghana-specific ANC visit schedule tracking
    *   Risk assessment & alerts
    *   Delivery outcome tracking
*   **Immunization Module:**
    *   Ghana EPI schedule tracking
    *   Immunization history & alerts
*   **NHIS Claims Export (Advanced):**
    *   Automated batch claims generation (G-DRG & Fee-for-Service)
    *   Claims status tracking & reconciliation
    *   Rejection reason management
*   **Referral Management:**
    *   Inbound & outbound referral tracking
    *   Automated referral letter generation
    *   NHIE referral submission
*   **Advanced Reporting:**
    *   DHIMS2 data export & integration
    *   Customizable dashboards for facility managers
    *   Disease surveillance reports

## Phase 3: Optimization & National Scale (Months 19-36+) - *Focus: Sustainability & Innovation*

**Objective:** Optimize system performance, introduce advanced features, and scale MedReg to support national deployment, fostering a vibrant ecosystem of local innovation and support.

### Key Deliverables:

*   **Decision Support Systems:**
    *   Clinical alerts & reminders (e.g., drug interactions, overdue immunizations)
    *   Ghana Standard Treatment Guidelines (STG) integration
*   **Patient Portal:**
    *   Secure access for patients to their health records
    *   Appointment scheduling & reminders
    *   Health education resources
*   **Telemedicine Integration:**
    *   Secure video consultation capabilities
    *   Remote monitoring integration
*   **Advanced Analytics & AI:**
    *   Predictive analytics for disease outbreaks
    *   AI-assisted diagnosis support
    *   Public health surveillance enhancements
*   **Multi-Facility Management:**
    *   Centralized user & facility administration
    *   Cross-facility reporting & analytics
*   **Ecosystem Development:**
    *   Open API for third-party integrations
    *   Developer training & certification program
    *   Local innovation challenges

## Our Commitment to Ghana

This roadmap is a living document, designed to adapt to the evolving needs of Ghana's healthcare system. Our commitment remains steadfast: to deliver a high-quality, sustainable, and Ghana-owned digital health solution that empowers healthcare providers and improves patient outcomes across the nation.
